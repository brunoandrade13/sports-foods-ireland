About High5

High5 is a UK-based sports nutrition brand with 25+ years of formulation experience. Their products are used by professional cycling teams, triathlon squads and everyday athletes. High5 is known for clean formulations, great taste and strong value — making it a popular choice for Irish athletes across all disciplines.

Sports Foods Ireland has been supplying Irish athletes with High5 products for years. We are an authorised stockist based in Blanchardstown, Dublin, delivering nationwide across Ireland. Our team uses these products in training and racing, so we can offer expert advice on what works best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy High5 in Ireland?

Sports Foods Ireland stocks the full High5 range in Ireland. Order online with nationwide delivery and free shipping on orders over €60.

What is High5 Energy Gel Aqua?

High5 Energy Gel Aqua is a thinner, more liquid energy gel that can be taken without water — ideal for running races and other activities where carrying water is difficult.

Is High5 Zero suitable as a daily electrolyte drink?

Yes. High5 Zero is a low-calorie electrolyte tablet designed to replace minerals lost through sweat. It's suitable as a daily hydration drink for active people and athletes.
