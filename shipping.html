🏢 B2B Wholesale Delivery

Wholesale customers enjoy free delivery on orders over €150. Standard shipping rate of €9.04 applies for B2B orders under €150. For bulk or pallet deliveries, please contact us for a custom quote.

🌍 Delivery Areas

🇮🇪 Republic of Ireland

We deliver to all counties across Ireland. Standard delivery times apply to most areas. Remote locations may take an additional 1-2 days.

🇬🇧 Northern Ireland

We also deliver to Northern Ireland. Same pricing and delivery times as Republic of Ireland.

📍 Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an email with:

  • ✓ Tracking number
  • ✓ Link to carrier's tracking page
  • ✓ Estimated delivery date
  • ✓ Delivery instructions if applicable

You can also track your order by logging into your account or contacting our customer service team.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I'm not home?

The courier will leave a card with instructions for redelivery or collection from a local depot.

Can I change my delivery address?

Yes, contact us within 2 hours of placing your order and we'll update the address if the order hasn't been dispatched.

Do you deliver on weekends?

Standard delivery is Monday-Friday. Express delivery is available for Saturday delivery at an additional cost.
