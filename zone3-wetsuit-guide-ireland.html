Zone3 Wetsuit Guide: Which Suit to Buy in Ireland

By Sports Foods Ireland Team · April 2026 · 7 min read

Zone3 is the UK's number one triathlon brand, and for good reason. Their wetsuits offer an exceptional performance-to-price ratio, making them a popular choice among Irish triathletes and open water swimmers at every level — from first-time sea swimmers to Ironman qualifiers.

Sports Foods Ireland is an official Zone3 stockist. Here's our honest guide to choosing the right Zone3 wetsuit for Irish conditions.

Zone3 Agile — Best for Beginners

The Zone3 Agile is Zone3's entry-level wetsuit and one of the best-selling wetsuits in Ireland. Despite being the most affordable suit in the range, it delivers genuine performance: 2mm, 3mm and 4mm panels provide core buoyancy, 2mm Flex-Fit shoulder panels allow easy arm movement, and high-visibility panels keep you safe in Irish waters. If this is your first wetsuit or you swim recreationally year-round, the Agile is the right choice.

Zone3 Advance — The Most Popular Irish Choice

The Zone3 Advance has been called "the undisputed best entry-level wetsuit" by multiple independent reviewers. A step up from the Agile, the Advance adds better buoyancy panels in the lower legs (helping keep your legs up in the water — critical for pool swimmers transitioning to open water), a smoother outer coating for reduced drag, and improved flexibility in the shoulders. The Advance is the most popular Zone3 model among Irish club triathletes.

Zone3 Vision — Best Mid-Range

Named Best Triathlon Wetsuit of 2024 by Triathlete.com, the Zone3 Vision bridges the gap between accessible and elite performance. SCS (Super Composite Skin) coating on the chest and shoulders reduces drag and makes donning and doffing faster — important in race transitions. The Vision is the choice for athletes looking to take a few minutes off their swim time without spending on a top-end suit.

Zone3 Aspire — For Competitive Athletes

The Zone3 Aspire is a multi-award-winning design that has defined Zone3's top-range performance for over 15 years. With 5mm buoyancy foam in the core, 1.5mm panels in the shoulders for maximum stroke freedom, and full SCS coating for minimal drag, the Aspire is built for athletes who want every second advantage in the water. Used by age-group winners at Irish national triathlon events.

Which Zone3 Suit for Irish Sea Swimming?

Ireland's sea water temperatures present specific challenges:

  • Summer (June-September, 12-16°C): Any Zone3 wetsuit is suitable. The Agile or Advance are ideal for casual swimmers.
  • Spring/Autumn (April-May, October, 9-12°C): Consider a thicker suit (Advance or above) combined with Zone3 neoprene gloves and a swim cap.
  • Winter swimming (Oct-March, 6-10°C): Zone3 makes dedicated cold-water wetsuits. Pair any suit with 5mm neoprene gloves, socks and a thermal swim cap.

Zone3 Accessories for Irish Swimmers

Beyond wetsuits, Zone3 makes a complete range of open water accessories stocked at Sports Foods Ireland: neoprene gloves, swim socks, thermal swim caps, safety tow floats (required at many Irish swimming events) and triathlon swimwear. We stock both men's and women's sizes across all products.
