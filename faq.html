Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, returns, and more.

Shipping & Delivery

We offer standard delivery (3-5 business days) and express delivery (1-2 business days) across Ireland. Free delivery is available on orders over €60. International shipping is also available - please contact us for rates.

Standard delivery within Ireland costs €9.04 for orders under €60. Orders over €60 qualify for free delivery. B2B wholesale customers get free delivery on orders over €150. Express delivery is available for €9.95.

Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery takes 1-2 business days. You will receive a tracking number once your order has been dispatched.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day returns policy. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in the same condition as when received. Free returns are available for orders over €60.

To return an item, please contact us at info@sportsfoodsireland.ie or call +353 1 840 0403. We'll provide you with a returns label and instructions. Items should be returned to: Unit 12 Northwest Centre, Northwest Business Park, Blanchardstown, Dublin D15 YC53.

Once we receive your returned item, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Products

Many of our products are suitable for vegetarians and vegans. Please check the product description and ingredient list for each item. You can also filter products by dietary requirements on our shop page.

We occasionally offer sample packs and trial sizes. Please check our promotions page or contact us directly to inquire about available samples.

Our team is here to help! You can contact us via email or phone for personalised advice. We also provide detailed product descriptions and usage guidelines on each product page.

Orders & Payment

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Klarna. All payments are processed securely through encrypted connections.

Klarna is a flexible payment service that lets you shop now and pay later. At checkout, simply select Klarna as your payment method and choose from three options: Pay in 3 (split your purchase into 3 interest-free instalments), Pay Later (pay up to 30 days after delivery), or Pay Now (pay immediately via bank transfer). Klarna is available to eligible customers and subject to approval.

Klarna is available for most orders on our website. Eligibility is determined by Klarna based on your account history and order details. Klarna is not available for B2B wholesale orders — business customers should use bank transfer or card payment instead.

Klarna's Pay in 3 and Pay Later options are completely interest-free when you pay on time. However, late payments may incur fees charged directly by Klarna. We recommend reviewing Klarna's terms and conditions before completing your purchase. Sports Foods Ireland does not charge any additional fee for using Klarna.

For any queries about your Klarna payment plan, instalments, or account, please contact Klarna directly through their app or at klarna.com/ie. If your issue relates to the order itself (wrong item, missing delivery, return), please contact us at info@sportsfoodsireland.ie and we'll resolve it promptly.

If you need to modify or cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible. We can usually accommodate changes if the order hasn't been dispatched yet. Contact us at info@sportsfoodsireland.ie or +353 1 840 0403.

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk orders. Please contact us with your requirements and we'll provide a custom quote. This is particularly useful for teams, clubs, or training groups.

B2B / Wholesale

Yes! We have a dedicated B2B wholesale programme for gyms, sports clubs, retailers, physiotherapy clinics, coaches, and other businesses. Our wholesale customers get access to exclusive ex-VAT pricing, a dedicated B2B portal, and priority support. To apply, please visit our B2B Portal or contact us directly.

Applying is easy. Visit our B2B Portal and register your business. Once your account is reviewed and approved, you'll gain immediate access to wholesale pricing and the full B2B order portal. Approval typically takes 1 business day. You can also email us at info@sportsfoodsireland.ie with your business details.

Wholesale pricing is available ex-VAT (23% VAT excluded) across our full product range. Discount levels depend on the product and order volume. B2B customers also benefit from free delivery on orders over €150 and access to exclusive bulk pricing tiers. Log in to the B2B Portal to view your personalised pricing.

There is no strict minimum order quantity for wholesale orders. However, free B2B delivery applies to orders over €150 (ex-VAT). For very large or pallet orders, please contact us for a custom quote and delivery arrangement.

B2B wholesale customers can pay by bank transfer (EFT), credit or debit card, or invoice (for approved accounts with agreed credit terms). Klarna is not available for wholesale orders. If you require invoice payment terms, please contact us to discuss your account setup.

Yes, all B2B orders come with a full VAT invoice. You can view and download your invoices at any time from the B2B Portal under your order history. If you need a copy of a past invoice, contact us at info@sportsfoodsireland.ie.

We do not currently offer drop shipping or white-label products. All products are sold under their original brand names. If you have a specific partnership proposal, please get in touch at info@sportsfoodsireland.ie and we'll be happy to discuss.

Account & Support

You can create an account during checkout or by clicking on the account icon in the header. Having an account allows you to track orders, save your address, and view order history.

Click on "My account" and then "Forgot password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password.

You can reach us via email at info@sportsfoodsireland.ie, by phone at +353 1 840 0403 (Monday-Thursday, 09:00-16:00), or by using the contact form on our Contact page. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
