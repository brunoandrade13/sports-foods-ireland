About PowerBar

Founded in 1986 by marathon runner Brian Maxwell, PowerBar pioneered the sports nutrition bar category. Today PowerBar serves professional and amateur athletes across cycling, running, triathlon and team sports. Their Ride and Run ranges are specifically formulated for endurance sports performance, with precisely calibrated carbohydrate ratios based on current sports science.

Sports Foods Ireland has been supplying Irish athletes with PowerBar products for years. We are an authorised stockist based in Blanchardstown, Dublin, delivering nationwide across Ireland. Our team uses these products in training and racing, so we can offer expert advice on what works best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy PowerBar in Ireland?

Sports Foods Ireland is an official PowerBar stockist for Ireland. We offer nationwide delivery with free shipping on orders over €60.

What PowerBar products are best for cycling?

The PowerBar Ride range — including Energize bars, C2MAX gels and IsoMax drink — is specifically designed for cycling. The C2MAX dual-source carbohydrate formula delivers more energy per hour than glucose alone.

What is the difference between PowerBar Energize and Natural Energy bars?

Energize bars use a maltodextrin and fructose blend for rapid energy delivery. Natural Energy bars use dates and oats for slower, more sustained energy — better before long efforts.
