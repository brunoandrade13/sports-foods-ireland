Returns & Refunds

Our hassle-free 30-day return policy

🔄 30-Day Money Back Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy for any reason, you can return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

📋 How to Return an Item

1

Contact Us

Email us at returns@sportsfoodsireland.ie or call our customer service to request a return.

2

Pack Your Item

Place the item in its original packaging (if possible) and include your order number.

3

Ship It Back

Send the package to our returns address. We'll email you a prepaid label for faulty items.

4

Get Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect the item, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

✅ What Can Be Returned

  • ✓ Unused items in original packaging
  • ✓ Items with tags still attached
  • ✓ Faulty or damaged items (contact us immediately)
  • ✓ Wrong items received
  • ✓ Clothing that doesn't fit (unworn, with tags)

❌ What Cannot Be Returned

  • ✗ Opened food/nutrition products (for hygiene reasons)
  • ✗ Used or worn items
  • ✗ Items without original packaging
  • ✗ Personalized or custom items
  • ✗ Items returned after 30 days

💰 Refund Information

Refund Method: Original payment method
Processing Time: 5-7 business days after receipt
Shipping Costs: Refunded for faulty/wrong items only
Exchange Option: Available for size/color changes
