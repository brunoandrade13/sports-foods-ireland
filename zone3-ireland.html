About Zone3

Founded by world-class triathlete James Locke, Zone3 designs wetsuits, swimwear and triathlon kit engineered for buoyancy, flexibility and race-day speed. Zone3 products are used by athletes from beginner open water swimmers to Ironman champions. Sports Foods Ireland has been a Zone3 stockist since the brand's early years in Ireland.

Sports Foods Ireland has been supplying Irish athletes with Zone3 products for years. We are an authorised stockist based in Blanchardstown, Dublin, delivering nationwide across Ireland. Our team uses these products in training and racing, so we can offer expert advice on what works best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy Zone3 in Ireland?

Sports Foods Ireland is an official Zone3 stockist in Ireland. We carry wetsuits, gloves, swim caps, tow floats and accessories with nationwide delivery.

What Zone3 wetsuit should I buy as a beginner?

The Zone3 Agile wetsuit is the best choice for beginners. It offers core buoyancy, warmth and flexibility at an entry-level price point. For slightly more performance, the Advance is the most popular entry model.

Does Zone3 make gear for open water swimming?

Yes. Zone3 designs products specifically for open water swimming, including wetsuits, neoprene gloves, swim socks, tow floats and brightly coloured swim caps for visibility.
