About Clif Bar

Founded in 1990 by Gary Erickson after a 175-mile bike ride, Clif Bar has become one of the world's most trusted sports nutrition brands. Made with up to 70% organic ingredients, each bar delivers a blend of carbohydrates, protein and fibre for sustained energy during extended activity. Popular with cyclists, runners, hikers and triathletes across Ireland.

Sports Foods Ireland has been supplying Irish athletes with Clif Bar products for years. We are an authorised stockist based in Blanchardstown, Dublin, delivering nationwide across Ireland. Our team uses these products in training and racing, so we can offer expert advice on what works best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy Clif Bar in Ireland?

Sports Foods Ireland is an official Clif Bar retailer in Ireland. We ship nationwide with free delivery on orders over €60.

Are Clif Bars suitable for cycling?

Yes. Clif Bars are ideal before and during long rides. The mix of carbohydrates, protein and fibre provides sustained energy for 2-3+ hours of cycling.

What is the difference between Clif Bar and Clif Shot Bloks?

Clif Bars are whole food energy bars best eaten before or during activity. Shot Bloks are chewable energy squares with fast-acting carbohydrates, ideal for consuming during intense exercise.
