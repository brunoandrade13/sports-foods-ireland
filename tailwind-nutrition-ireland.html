About Tailwind Nutrition

Tailwind Nutrition was developed by endurance athletes who wanted a simpler fuelling solution. The result is a drink mix that provides complete nutrition during long efforts — no digestive issues, no flavour fatigue, no bonking. Tailwind has become a favourite among Ironman athletes, ultramarathon runners and long-distance cyclists worldwide.

Sports Foods Ireland has been supplying Irish athletes with Tailwind Nutrition products for years. We are an authorised stockist based in Blanchardstown, Dublin, delivering nationwide across Ireland. Our team uses these products in training and racing, so we can offer expert advice on what works best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy Tailwind Nutrition in Ireland?

Sports Foods Ireland is the official Tailwind retailer in Ireland. We stock all Tailwind products with free delivery on orders over €60.

How do I use Tailwind Endurance Fuel?

Mix 2 scoops of Tailwind per 500ml of water. For endurance events, consume 150-200 calories (2 scoops) per hour. No other nutrition is needed during exercise when using Tailwind correctly.

Is Tailwind suitable for cycling and running?

Tailwind is used by cyclists, runners, triathletes and ultramarathon athletes. Its liquid form makes it easier to consume during intense exercise than solid foods.
