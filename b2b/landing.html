Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order value?
There is no fixed minimum order value. However, some products have minimum quantity requirements which are clearly displayed in the wholesale portal.
How long does approval take?
Most applications are reviewed within 24–48 business hours. You'll receive an email notification once your account has been approved.
Are B2B prices exclusive of VAT?
Yes, all prices shown in the B2B wholesale portal are exclusive of VAT. VAT will be calculated at checkout based on your location and VAT registration.
What is the delivery policy for B2B?
Free delivery on wholesale orders over €150. Standard shipping of €9.04 applies for orders under €150. Next-day and express delivery options are also available.
Can I backorder out-of-stock items?
Yes! B2B customers can place backorders on any out-of-stock item. We'll fulfil the order as soon as stock arrives and notify you by email.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Stripe (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Apple Pay, Google Pay) and PayPal for B2B orders. All transactions are processed securely.
