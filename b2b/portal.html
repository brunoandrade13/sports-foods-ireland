🎧 B2B Support

Need help? Our dedicated B2B support team is available on weekdays.

📦 Orders

How do I place a B2B order?+
Log in to your B2B portal, go to the B2B Shop tab, add products to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can also use the "New Order" shortcut on the dashboard.
What is the minimum order value?+
There is no fixed minimum order value for B2B customers. However, free delivery applies to orders over €150.
Can I modify or cancel an order after placing it?+
Contact our team as soon as possible. We can modify or cancel orders that haven't been dispatched yet. Once dispatched, you'll need to follow our returns process.
Can I place a backorder for out-of-stock items?+
Yes! B2B customers can backorder any out-of-stock product. We'll fulfil the order as soon as stock arrives and notify you by email.

🚚 Delivery & Shipping

What are the delivery times?+
Standard delivery is 1–3 business days within Ireland. Express and next-day delivery options are available at checkout.
What is the delivery cost for B2B?+
Free delivery on B2B orders over €150. Standard shipping of €9.04 applies for orders under €150.
Do you deliver outside Ireland?+
Yes, we deliver across the UK and EU. Contact our team for international shipping rates and estimated delivery times.

🔄 Returns & Exchanges

What is the returns policy for B2B?+
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unopened products in original packaging. Contact our team to initiate a return and receive a return authorisation number.
Can I exchange a product for a different size/flavour?+
Yes, exchanges are available for unopened products. Contact us with your order number and the replacement item you'd like.

🛡️ Warranty

What products are covered by warranty?+
All wetsuits (Openwater and Triathlon) and electronics (bike computers, sensors, lights) come with a 12-month manufacturer warranty covering manufacturing defects.
How do I make a warranty claim?+
Open a Support ticket with photos of the defect, your order number, and date of purchase. Our team will review and process the claim within 5 business days.

💳 Payments

What payment methods do you accept for B2B?+
We accept Stripe (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Apple Pay, Google Pay), PayPal, and bank transfer. All prices in the B2B portal are shown ex-VAT.
What are the payment terms?+
Standard payment terms are Net 30 days from invoice date. For alternative arrangements, contact our accounts team at accounts@sportsfoodsireland.ie.
Can I get a VAT invoice?+
Yes, all B2B orders automatically generate a VAT invoice which you can download from the Financial section of your portal.

ℹ️ General B2B

How do I update my company information?+
Go to the "My Account" tab in your B2B portal to update your company details, address, and contact information.
Can I add multiple users to my B2B account?+
Currently each B2B account has one login. Contact us if you need multiple user access and we'll set up additional accounts linked to your company.
