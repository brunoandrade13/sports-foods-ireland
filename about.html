About Sports Foods Ireland

Our Story

Sports Foods Ireland was founded with a passion for supporting athletes at every level. We understand that peak performance requires the right nutrition, quality equipment, and expert guidance. That's why we've curated a comprehensive range of products from leading brands, ensuring you have access to everything you need to excel in your sport.

Based in Dublin, we serve athletes across Ireland and beyond, providing professional-grade sports nutrition, cycling gear, swimming equipment, and running accessories. Our commitment to quality and customer service has made us a trusted name in the Irish sports community.

Our Mission

To empower athletes of all levels by providing premium sports nutrition and equipment, backed by expert knowledge and exceptional customer service. We believe that every athlete deserves access to the best products to help them reach their goals.

Our Values

Quality

We only stock products from trusted, reputable brands that meet the highest standards of quality and performance.

Expertise

Our team has extensive knowledge of sports nutrition and equipment, ready to help you find the perfect products for your needs.

Service

Customer satisfaction is our priority. We offer free delivery over €60, 30-day returns, and dedicated support.

Community

We're proud to support the Irish sports community, from weekend warriors to elite athletes.

Why Choose Us?

At Sports Foods Ireland, we offer:

  • Extensive range of products from top brands including PowerBar, High 5, Zone 3, Spatzwear, and more
  • Free delivery on orders over €60
  • 30-day returns policy for your peace of mind
  • Secure payment processing
  • Expert advice and customer support
  • Fast and reliable shipping across Ireland
