Cookie Policy

How we use cookies to improve your experience

Last updated: January 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

🔒 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like shopping cart functionality and secure checkout.

cart_session Stores your shopping cart contents
csrf_token Security token for form submissions

📊 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website, which pages are most popular, and how users navigate the site.

_ga Google Analytics - tracks website usage
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users

🎯 Functionality Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and provide enhanced features.

recently_viewed Stores recently viewed products
wishlist Stores your wishlist items

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Here's how:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality, including the shopping cart and checkout process.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services that appear on our pages:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics
  • Stripe/PayPal: For secure payment processing
  • Social Media: If you use social sharing buttons

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
